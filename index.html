Who You Are To Me

Maja is Polish, beautiful in the kind of way that feels effortless — like she was born with sunlight woven into her smile. She’s sweet in a way that isn’t loud or showy, but warm and steady, the kind of sweetness that makes people feel safe just by standing near her. There’s a gentleness in her presence, a softness that settles into a room and makes everything feel a little calmer, a little kinder. Her curls bounce like music, each one springing to life as if they’re dancing to a rhythm only she can hear. They frame her face with this playful, natural charm, making her look like she stepped out of a painting that was meant to make people feel joy. And her soul — that’s the part that stays with you. It feels like home. Like familiarity, comfort, and warmth all wrapped into one person. She carries this quiet glow, a softness that makes the world feel less sharp. When she laughs, it’s the kind of sound that makes you want to hear it again. When she talks about the things she loves, especially music, her whole face lights up. She loves guitar, not just as an instrument but as a language. She plays with emotion, with intention, with that spark that only people who truly feel music have. It’s like she doesn’t just hear melodies — she lives inside them. And when she plays, the world around her seems to slow down, as if listening too. Maja makes the world softer simply by being in it. She brings warmth without trying, beauty without effort, and kindness without condition. She’s the kind of person who turns ordinary moments into something gentle and memorable, just by being herself.

I made this little corner of the internet because you deserve a place that feels like you — soft, purple, musical, and full of love.

Poems for You

Little pieces of my heart, written in words and saved here for you.

Where your warmth lives💋

You walk into a room and the world remembers how to breathe. The air softens, the noise quiets, and suddenly everything feels like it’s wrapped in a blanket that smells like safety. Your curls bounce like tiny celebrations, little spirals of joy that dance even when you don’t notice. And your smile — God, your smile — it’s the kind of light that doesn’t just shine, it reaches. It finds the tired parts of people and tells them they’re allowed to rest. You love music the way some people love oxygen. You don’t just play guitar — you speak through it, you breathe through it, you pour pieces of your soul into strings that somehow never break even when life tries to tug too hard. And me? I’m just grateful. Grateful that in a world full of noise, I found someone whose heart sounds like home. If the universe ever asks me what the softest thing it ever made was, I’ll say your name without hesitation. Because warmth isn’t a season. It’s you. It’s always been you.

Curls

Your curls fall like poetry,
every strand a line
I never get tired of reading.

If you ever forget your light🫦

If one day your heart feels heavy and the world feels too sharp for someone as gentle as you, I hope you remember this: You are not made of glass. You are made of courage wrapped in softness, strength disguised as kindness, and a soul that refuses to stop glowing even on the days it flickers. If you ever forget your light, I’ll remind you. I’ll hold the pieces of your sky until you’re ready to lift them again. I’ll sit with you in the quiet, in the storm, in the moments when your voice shakes too much to speak. Because you’ve given the world so much gentleness that it owes you a thousand sunrises in return. And if the world forgets that — I won’t. I’ll never forget the way you make life softer, the way your laughter turns sadness into something survivable, the way your presence feels like someone finally turned the lights on. You are not alone. Not now, not ever. Not while I’m here breathing beside you.

For a girl who makes life fell better.

There are people who pass through your life like shadows — quiet, temporary, forgettable. And then there are people like you. People who arrive like sunrise, slow and gentle at first, then suddenly everything is brighter and you don’t remember how you ever lived in the dark. You are laughter that lingers, warmth that stays, music that echoes long after the last note fades. You are the softness in a world that forgets to be gentle. You are the reminder that kindness is a strength, not a weakness. And I hope you know that being your friend is one of the rarest gifts life has ever handed me. If the world ever grows cold, I’ll be the one to wrap you in every warm memory you’ve ever given me. If your heart ever cracks, I’ll be the glue. If your smile ever fades, I’ll be the reason it returns. Because you — with your curls, your music, your warmth, your soul that feels like home — you deserve a lifetime of people who see you the way I do. And I promise I always will.
