Who You Are To Me

Maja is Polish, beautiful, sweet, and full of warmth. She has curls that bounce like music and a soul that feels like home. She loves guitar, she loves music, and she makes the world softer just by being in it.

I made this little corner of the internet because you deserve a place that feels like you — soft, purple, musical, and full of love.

Poems for You

Little pieces of my heart, written in words and saved here for you.

Soft

You move through the world
like a quiet song —
soft, warm,
and impossible to forget.

Curls

Your curls fall like poetry,
every strand a line
I never get tired of reading.

Purple

If you were a colour,
you’d be the softest shade of purple —
gentle, glowing,
and full of magic.

Guitar

When you play,
it feels like the world pauses
just to listen
to the softness in your hands.

Home

Being near you
feels like coming home
to a place I’ve never been
but always needed.

Night

You make the night feel young,
like the world is still glowing
and anything could happen
as long as you’re here.
